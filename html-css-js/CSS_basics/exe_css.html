Recipe: Crape

Description: How do you make crepes at home?

Ingredients

  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 cup 3% milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 cup (or 140 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 canola oil spray

Filling
1 jar of Nutella

Instructions
Blend the eggs, milk, sugar, oil, and flour in a blender for about 30 seconds until smooth. If small lumps of flour remain, strain the batter. Heat a crepe pan (or a wide, flat pan) and lightly grease it with canola oil spray. Pour about 1/2 cup of the batter into the pan and swirl it in a circular motion to spread the batter evenly and thinly. Cook on both sides until very lightly golden.
